# Privacy Policy for Brain Squared Extension

**Last Updated:** November 8, 2025

## Overview
Brain Squared Extension helps you save and sync your browsing history to your personal knowledge base.

## Data Collection
We collect:
- Your browsing history (URLs, titles, visit times, visit counts)
- Only when you explicitly click "Sync to Brain Squared"

## Data Usage
- Your browsing data is sent to your Brain Squared backend server
- Data is used solely for your personal knowledge management
- We do NOT share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties

## Data Storage
- Data is stored on your personal Brain Squared server
- You have full control over your data
- You can delete your data at any time

## Permissions
The extension requires:
- **history**: To read your browsing history
- **downloads**: To export history files
- **storage**: To remember sync settings
- **alarms**: For automatic syncing
- **notifications**: To notify you of sync status

## Third-Party Services
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- No external services except your own Brain Squared server
